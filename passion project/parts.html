Parts & People of the firefighting system

Agency. Who manages firefighting?

Fire fighting agencies from the federal government (Dept. of the Interior) and state governement (CalFire).

Who does the firefighting? See for yourself.

People become firefighters, and work for the agencies. The training is aduous, and the pay is low. Currently there is a shortage of firefighters, specifically those who are trained to fight wildfires.

What is a "private firefighter?" Why are they here.

Private firefighters have emerged to fill the gaps that fire agencies are currently suffering from. They have trouble finding new firefighters because the training takes a long time and the pay is relatively low. Private firefighters provide expertiese to the federal and state agencies for cheaper than hiring new firefighters.
